Frequently and Funny Asked Questions


  • What is the best way to contact you?
  • Please use the contact form above, I will try my best to get back to you within two business days.


  • WHY IS THE STORE NOT UP?
  • Oopsie! I blame Butterbee! Go ahead and shoot me a message!


  • Do you have any advice about getting into animation/music/writing?
  • It's all about practice! I used to be a tonedeaf, (still dyslexic), child that can't draw a horse without it look like a fat pig...Never give up! Pratice everyday, learn new skills and techniques. Then one day, my friend, you will also be stuck in your room all day creating things!


  • I love what your doing! How can I support you?
  • Just keep on watching videos, give me a like/subscribe, bother me to get merch, and let me know what you'd like to see more of!


  • Butterbee Questions:

  • How did you create Butterbee?
  • Well, Butterbee is actually just how I view myself but the character design was a very difficult process. I started with a rough sketch and just kept re-drawing until I finally got a bit closer to what I really wanted. Safe to say many pieces of paper, pencils, and sandwiches lost their lives that day. RIP.


  • Alan is my favorite! Who is he based on?
  • My little brother. He is a sweet guy, but his death stare could kill a man.


  • Novel Questions:

  • What inspires you to write?
  • Oddly enough, most of my novel ideas start as dreams. The rest comes from my life and those around me.


  • Your dyslexic? How do you manage to write novels?
  • Well since I was home-schooled, my mother pushed both me and my older brother really hard when it came to reading. She never believed it was an excuse, just an opportunity to work harder!


  • Wait, you were home-schooled? Doesn't that mean your like socially awkward or something?
  • Despite common belief, I do not have any problem with social interaction, schedules, or commitment. I was forced to work very hard, started college at 13 with a 3.9GPA, and some met some great people from all over.


  • Music Questions:

  • How long have you been songwriting?
  • I have been writing song since I was three, of course, they were awful and all about my love of cats.


  • How many songs have you written so far?
  • I have over 100 completed songs and many more in the works.


  • How long have you been playing guitar?
  • I bought my first guitar at 16, told myself I'm gonna learn this thing! That day, I spent 6 hours playing two chords over and over again. By the end of it, I couldn't feel my fingertips for 2 weeks. I really thought that I had murdered my hand.


  • How long have you been playing ukulele?
  • Not even a year! (2019) I thought they were adorbale, bought one, the rest was history.

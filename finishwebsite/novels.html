Nethermore
On the outskirts of a murderous forest lived a girl named Snow. She is forced to wander into the forest when she discovers her best friend has gone missing. Little did she know, that choice would destroy her life. Piece by piece, she uncovers dark secrets from her past, she was abandoned as a baby, her biological parents are killer, her friends are not what they seem, and one handsome stranger is the key to it all.
Nethermore is my first young adult fiction novel at 78,000 words, currently looking for representation

Dear Mr. Somebody
Think dating in the new age is hard? Well try being a 24 year old, borderline asexual woman that is completely against having kids or marriage, but still hold on hope to finding her better half. Luckily, fate is on her side and her life takes an unexpected turn when she meets Fin, a 26 year old weirdo that’s still trying to get his life together, who completely steals her heart. Between the childish shenanigans, hilarious misunderstanding, morbid jokes, borderline psycho dates, a very involved and super religious mother, and their two wacky friends, it’s a insane miracle these two found each other.
Dear Mr. Somebody is an adult comedic romance novel, coming soon.

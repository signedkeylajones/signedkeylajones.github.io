What is a Butterbee?

Meet Butterbee, an adorable green little creature who tends to cause a lot of trouble, due to his childish and stubborn attitude.
Alan, his guardian fairy, spends most of his time trying to get Butterbee out of trouble though it's not easy. Throughout the series, the two have wacky adventures, meet a variety of interesting creatures, and even sometimes break into song...for no reason.

Butterbee is an original animated series by Keyla Jones & Picture Purrfect Studios.

Meet the Cast:

Butterbee is voiced by Keyla Jones

Keyla also voices characters such as, Gwen, Goi Goi, Onyon, and Lov-a-bee.

Alan is voiced by Andrew Gutekanst, who was also the inspiration for the character.

Mermaid Marri is voiced by Connie Gutekanst.

Evil Wizard Bluu is voiced by Stephen Gutekanst.

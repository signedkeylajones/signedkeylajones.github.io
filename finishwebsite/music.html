My Adventure

Hai! I'm a singer-songwriter, self taught ukuleleist & guitarist, novelist, fake mustache enthusist, and animator of my original cartoon series, Butterbee. I know that sounds really crazy so let me explain how this all happened.

It all started back in Phoenix, AZ, where I was homeschooled with my three brothers. While they were dismantiling any sort of electric and teaching themselves to program. I was writing books, new songs, cartoons (horses that looked like fat pigs), or singing my heart out (horribly I might add). I was kind of the black-sheep of my family. I was raised by two engineers and three computer science brothers. Safe to say, they got the book smarts, I got the crayons.

Growing up I suffered from the "What am I doing with my life?" illness and thus contantly pushing myself to work hard on the things I love. I was a professional gymnest at age 6, a competitive cheerleader at age 9 (I was thrown into the air...), a theater and independant film actor at age 15, an award-winning playwrite and director at age 16, a traveling/preforming singer-songwriter at age 18, a legally different person because of a name change at age 19, a Marriage officiant at age 20, a novelist at age 21, and a voice actor, animator of a cartoon series at age 23.

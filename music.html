History

Keyla Jones was homeschooled with her three brothers in her hometown of Phoenix, AZ. She has dyslexia so her mother/teacher encouraged her to work on the things she loved. She began writing songs at the age of three and hasn't stopped a day since. 
She bought her first guitar at sixteen years old and taught herself how to play through the power of the internet and Youtube.
Since then she has performed at many different venues across Arizona and Washington State, including, Arizona State Fair, Mesa Music Festival, Making a Difference Day for kids with disabilities, Rock the Dock, etc.

Keyla is a self-taught songwriter, Guitarist, Ukuleleist, and Pianist.

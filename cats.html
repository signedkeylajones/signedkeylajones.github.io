Picture Purrfect Studios

Picture Purrfect Studios was created by Keyla Jones as a place to showcase all of her artwork. The name and animation was inspired by ten beautiful cats, nine of which are her brothers.

Continue onward to learn about each kitty.

The Kitties

Bowtie

Bow-tie, much like his country name, is the friendly little critter you'll meet. He loves everyone and and to be held upside down. If I didn't know better, I would say he is a dog..

Lefty

Lefty is...something? A little bit of a weirdo and a little bit of a cuddler all mixed into one. He likes to snuggle, attack you when you least expect it, jump onto high places and then scream because he can't get down, and do superman moves while you hold him upside down. <3

TipToe

Tip-toe is light on his feet so therefore he attemps the most dangerous stunts known to cat-kind. Jumping onto tall heights? Not a problem. Getting stuck inside a closet? You know it. Trying to jump onto a ceiling fan? Well someone has to try...right?

Rudolph

He'll light your way in a cold winters day with his kitty powers! He's a true kitty cat, he keeps to himself, and welcomes all kitties to snuggle with him. He's a little love-bug that just wants to be held all the time ...and by that I mean, I want to hold him all the time.

Puff

He's a fur ball. He full of cuddles, hair in your mouth, adorable moments, hair in your eyeballs, naps, and clothes covered in black hair.

Aria

Aria is the only girl from a litter of five boys. She is an adorable tiny little cat that can be skittish at time, but loves attention (expect from woman).

Mama Bear

It all started during an Arizona heat wave, My brother wanted to help her and her babbies, but this strong kitty woman, refused, HARD. After some oven mits, multiple failed attempts, and some new warrior scars (to my brother). Momma loved her new extravagent life style with food/water, toys, and an air-conditioned house.

Bubbles

Bubbles like to ride solo, with six kittens constantly following his lead. He was a loner, who one day walked into my brothers life (litterally), decided he like the joint, and stayed for life. The rest is history.

Nala

Nala was a beauitful and very violent cat to everyone but my brother (She was a little less violent to him.) Nala was his very first cat and made him a permanent cat lover for life.
R.I.P. Nala

Cass

I found Cass when I was about three years old, since that day, he became my best friend. I used to call Cass my abusive lover because despite biting my toes in the middle of the night, loudly moewing because he wanted me to wake up, and randomly attacking me when I was petting him, I loved him more than anyone could imagine. Cass was a boy, but I always called him a girl because I have cat gender issues. P.S. I know you still bite my toes in the middle of the night, even as a ghost. R.I.P. Cassie

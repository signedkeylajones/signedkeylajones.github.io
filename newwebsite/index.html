Butterbee is an original animated series by Keyla Jones & Picture Purrfect Studios. The story follows a green little creature called, Butterbee, who tends to cause a lot of trouble, due to his childish and stubborn attitude. Alan, his guardian fairy, spends most of his time trying to get Butterbee out of trouble though it's not easy. Throughout the series, the two have wacky adventures, meet a variety of interesting creatures, and even sometimes break into song...for no reason. Want to hear more? Click here!

Keyla Jones is an inspiring writer with one finished novel and three more in the works. Her first novel follows a girl named Snow, who lives on the outskirts of a murderous forest called, Nethermore. When she discovers her best friend has gone missing, she is forced to wander into the forest. Little did she knew, this choice would destroy her entire life. Piece by piece, she uncovers dark secrets from her past, she was abandoned as a baby, her biological parents are killers, her friends are not what they seem, and one handsome stranger is the key to it all. Signed Keyla Jones is an original singer-songwriter known for her Spanish sound, powerful vocals, and beautiful lyrics that take you on a journey through tragic tales and inspiring love songs. Her music is inspired by real stories from her life and those of her loved ones. She has notably preformed at Arizona State Fair, Mesa Music Festival, Making a Difference Benefit Concert, and made the top four in the Rising Star of Tacoma contest. Listen to her music here!

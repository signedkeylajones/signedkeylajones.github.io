Signed Keyla Jones

"A Spicy Original Singer-Songwriter"

Local Tacoma Musician, Signed Keyla Jones, is a spicy original singer-songwriter known for her powerful vocals and beautiful original songs that take you on journeys through tragic tales and inspiring love songs.

Her unique music style was inspired by her love of Maroon 5, Colbie Caillat, and the Plain White T’s, and her unique lyrics are inspired by the stories of her life and those of her loved ones.

Keyla is a completely self-taught guitarist and has been writing songs since the young age of three. She has performed all over the Phoenix, AZ, area, including the Arizona State Fair and Mesa Music Festival. She has also performed locally at several venues and recently made the top four in the Raising Star of Tacoma Contest.

“Isn’t it just the best feeling when a song understands your mood perfectly?”

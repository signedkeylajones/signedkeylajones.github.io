Happy Foods - Rebranding Project

Happy Home Brew Coffee, an organic and locally made coffee blend, wanted to showcase it’s brand in a way that would capture it’s healthy, friendly, and new-age appeal.

The clean, non-fussy logo I designed reinforces the brand’s use of all-natural, organic ingredients and it’s simple to make instructions.
